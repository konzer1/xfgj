How often do you update your mods?

We update our mods as soon as possible after game updates are released. For popular games, updates are typically available within 24-48 hours. You can check the status of each mod on its respective page.

Do I need to disable my antivirus to use your mods?

Some antivirus programs may flag game mods as potentially unwanted programs due to their nature. This is a false positive. You may need to add an exception in your antivirus for our mods to work properly. We guarantee that our mods are free from actual malware or viruses.

If you encounter any issues, please check our troubleshooting guide first. If that doesn't resolve your problem, you can contact our support team through the Contact Us page or join our Discord community for real-time assistance.

Can I request a mod for a specific game?

Yes, we welcome mod requests for games not currently in our catalog. Please use the Contact Us page to submit your request. While we can't guarantee that we'll develop a mod for every requested game, we do consider all requests and prioritize those with high demand.
